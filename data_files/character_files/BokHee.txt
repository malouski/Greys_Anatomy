She is a common feature in most surgeries, but speaks very little. However, she has been shown to speak up when needed, as demonstrated when she suggested bypass during [[James Evans|Jimmy Evans']] surgery. BokHee has also been a help to most of the major surgeons in the hospital,\u00a0[[Preston Burke]]\u00a0is a prime example.
